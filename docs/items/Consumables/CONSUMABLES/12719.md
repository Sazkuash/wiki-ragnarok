# Oblivion Curse (ID: 12719)

### Description
    An ash powder that causes the Oblivion status.
    Oblivion lasts 300 seconds. Under the state of Oblivion, SP does not recover naturally and cannot be canceled by Lex Divina or Green Potion.
    You cannot use skills while affected by the ashes of Oblivion.
