# Red Booster (ID: 12796)

### Description
    A magical accelerator that activates bodily energy for better combat.
    Increases ATK/MATK by 30,
    Increases Attack Speed (decreases After Attack Delay by 5%),
    Decreases Variable Cast Time by 5%
    and increases MaxHP/MaxSP by 10% for 10 minutes.
    When attacking, Concentration Level 3 will be cast with a certain chance.
    (If the character has the skill at a Level greater than 3, the skill will be cast at the achieved Level)
    When the player
    Follow the NPC below to create the True Red Accelerator.
