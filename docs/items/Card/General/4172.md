# The Paper Card (ID: 4172)

## Description
> Increases Critical Damage by 20%.  
> Drains 1 SP per attack.  
> When equipped with Wanderer Card, Wild Rose Card, Shinobi Card and Zhu Po Long Card:  
> AGI +5  
> STR +5  
> Increases Attack Speed (decreases After-Attack Delay by 5%).  
> Increases movement speed.  
> Restores 1 SP per attack.  
> Disables the Auto-Intimidate skill of the Wanderer Card  
> When equipped by Thief classes:  
> Nullifies the Gem requirement for certain skills.  
> Type: Card  
> Combine into: Weapon  
> Weight: 1
