# Long Horn (ID: 1420)

## Description
> A spear made from a unicorn  
> Allows the use of Detoxify Level 1.  
> Random chance to inflict the Bleeding status on the target when dealing physical damage.  
> Indestructible in battle  
> When equipped with Valkyrie Shield:  
> Random chance to automatically cast Assumptio Level 2 on the user when receiving physical damage.  
> Type: One-Handed Spear  
> Attack: 150  
> Weight: 100  
> Element: Holy  
> Weapon Level: 4  
> Requirement:  
> Base Level 65  
> Transcendent Swordsman classes
