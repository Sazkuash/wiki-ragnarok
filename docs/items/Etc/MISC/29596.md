# Eremes (ID: 29596)

### Description
    For every 5 Refine Levels of the total Refine Level of Judgement Slasher +Repent Slasher:
    Increases the damage of Meteor Assault and Soul Destroyer by 10%
    Increases the damage of Cross Impact and Counter Attack by 5%.
    For every 3 Refinement Levels of Agudo Filo:
    Decreases the cooldown of the Shadow Claw skill by 3 seconds.
    Increases Attack Speed (decreases After Attack Delay by 2%).
    For every 3 Refine Levels of Reaper Cross:
    Increases the damage of Rolling Cutter by 7%.
    Increases damage of Cross Impact by 5%.
