# Heroic Desocketing Book (ID: 6484)

### Description
    Take this book and 1 Slotting Announcement to Troy on the second floor of the Eden Group Headquarters and he will remove all cards from 1 equipped item in your inventory.
    All cards will be returned, but the item where they were slotted will be destroyed forever.
    1 Book is required for each card that is removed.
    Weight: 1
