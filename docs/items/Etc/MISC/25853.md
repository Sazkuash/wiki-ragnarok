# Kagerou Stone(Garment) (ID: 25853)

### Description
    A stone that awakens the potential of Kagerous.
    Grants the following effects when equipped in the slot of a Costume Garment.
    Increases the damage of Spinning Petal by 25%.
    When equipped with Ninja Stone(Upper):
    Increases the damage of Cross Slash by 25%.
    When equipped with Ninja Stone (Middle):
    Increases Kunai Splash damage by 25%.
    When equipped with Ninja Stone(Lower):
    Physical attacks have a 1% chance to absorb 1% of the damage dealt as SP.
    Weight: 10
