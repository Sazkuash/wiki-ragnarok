    Increases the damage of Rolling Cutter by 15%.
    When equipped with Assassin Stone(Upper):
    Physical attacks have a 2% chance to absorb 1% of the damage dealt as SP.
    When equipped with Assassin Stone (Middle):
    Increases the damage of Cross Ripper Slasher by 15%.
    When equipped with Executioner Stone (Lower):
    Increases Rolling Cutter damage by an additional 15%.
