# Doram Stone(Garment) (ID: 25862)

### Description
    A stone that awakens the potential of Dorams.
    Grants the following effects when equipped in the slot of a Costume Garment.
    Increases the damage of Capricious Bite and Herb Meteor by 20%.
    When equipped with Doram Stone(Upper):
    Increases the damage of Lunatic Carrot Beat and Cat Apple Lance by 20%.
    When equipped with Doram Stone(Middle):
    Reduces the cooldown of the skill Wild Spirit by 1 second.
    When equipped with Doram Stone(Lower):
    Reduces the cooldown of the skill Herb Meteor by 1 second.
    Weight: 10
