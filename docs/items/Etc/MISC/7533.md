# Travel Brochure [Ayothaya] (ID: 7533)

### Description
    [Ayothaya]
    Thoughts of transcending your limitations and finding the perfect philosophy of Life are the heritage of Ayothaya.
    In the history of Ayothaya there is a great Philosopher that all Ayothayans seek to emulate.
    There are countless statues representing this Philosopher in and around Ayothaya.
    You must visit them to see the difference and meaning that these Reclining Philosopher Statues have for you.
    Part of the Philosophy involves many hours of meditation per day to maintain balance in your mind and body.
    You must visit the Shrine in Ayothaya and meditate on the journey of your life.
    And when you complete your Exercise and Introspection regime, you can replenish your body by visiting
    the Tom Yum Goong restaurant. The famous soup made here is truly delicious.
