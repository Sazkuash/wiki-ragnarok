# Prison (ID: 29300)

### Description
    Restores 100% HP and SP upon being resurrected.
    When equipped by Rune Knight:
    Increases the damage of Storm Impact by 100%.
    When equipped by Royal Guard:
    Reduces the cooldown of the Cannon Spear skill by 1 second.
    When equipped by Arch Mage:
    Reduces the cooldown of the skill Crimson Rock by 1.5 seconds.
    When equipped by Sorcerer:
    Reduces Variable Cast Time by 15%.
    When equipped by Ranger:
    Increases the damage of Warg Strike by 30%.
    When equipped by Minstrel/Wanderer:
    All Basic Stats +10
    When equipped by Archbishop:
    Allows the use of Providence Level 5.
    When equipped by Sura:
    Increases Ranged Physical Damage by 10%.
    When equipped by Assassin Cross:
    Reduces After-Skill Delay by 90%.
    When using Shadow Step and being in the state of Weapon Blocking:
    The Weapon Blocking state will be replaced by a Weapon Blocking state with a high chance to parry attacks for 50 seconds.
    When equipped by Rebel:
    Reduces Fixed Cast Time by 50%.
    When equipped by Mechanic:
    Increases Attack Speed (decreases After-Attack Delay by 20%).
    When equipped by Genetic:
    MaxHP +15%
    ATK +50, MATK +50
