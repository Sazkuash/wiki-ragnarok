# Star Emperor Stone(Garment) (ID: 25849)

### Description
    A stone that awakens the potential of Star Emperors.
    Grants the following effects when equipped in a Visual Garment slot.
    Increases the damage of Falling Star, Solar Burst and Full Moon Kick by 20%.
    When equipped with Stellar Punisher Stone (Upper):
    Increases Solar Burst damage by an additional 15%.
    When equipped with Stellar Hunter Stone (Middle):
    Increases the damage of Falling Star by an additional 15%.
    When equipped with Stellar Hunter Stone (Lower):
    Physical attacks have a 2% chance to absorb 1% of the damage dealt as SP.
    Weight: 10
