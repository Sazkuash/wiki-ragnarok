# Gertie (ID: 29603)

### Description
    For every 3 Refine Levels of Jack the Knife:
    Increases the damage of Back Stab by 10%.
    Increases damage of Fatal Menace by 5%.
    For every 3 Refinement Levels of Platinum Dagger:
    Increases Magic Damage of Fire, Water, Wind and Earth elements by 5%.
    Increases Attack Speed (reduces After-Attack Delay by 2%).
    For every 3 Refinement Levels of Rapid Fire:
    Increases damage of Triangle Shot by 7%.
    Increases Ranged Physical Damage by 2%.
