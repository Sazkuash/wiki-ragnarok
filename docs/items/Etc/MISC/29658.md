# Star Emperor Stone(Garment) (ID: 29658)

### Description
    Increases the damage of Shooting Star, Solar Explosion and Full Moon Kick by 20%.
    When equipping with Taekwon Master Stone (Upper):
    Increases the damage of Solar Explosion by an additional 15%.
    When equipped with Taekwon Master Stone (Mid):
    Increases Falling Star damage by an additional 15%.
    When equipped with Taekwon Master Stone (Lower):
    Physical attacks have a 2% chance to absorb 1% of the damage dealt as SP.
