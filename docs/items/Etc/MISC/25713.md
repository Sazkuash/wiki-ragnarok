# Royal Guard Stone(Garment) (ID: 25713)

### Description
    A stone that awakens the potential of Royal Guards.
    Grants the following effects when combined in a slot of a Costume Garment.
    Increases the damage of Banishing Point by 15%.
    When equipped with Paladin Stone(Upper):
    Physical attacks have a 2% chance to absorb 1% of the damage dealt as SP.
    When equipped with Paladin Stone (Middle):
    Reduces the skill cooldown of Cannon Spear by 0.5 seconds.
    When equipped with Paladin Stone (Lower):
    Increases Banishing Point damage by an additional 15%.
    Weight: 10
