# Valkyrie Shoes (ID: 2421)

## Description

> These shoes become more powerful as the user improves their own abilities.

> MaxHP +(Base Level*5).  
> When equipped by the classes Swordsman, Merchant and ^0033CThief:  
> MaxSP +(Class Level*2)  
> Indestructible in battle  
> Type: Shoes  
> Defense: 13  
> Weight: 50  
> Armor Level: 4  
> Requirement:
