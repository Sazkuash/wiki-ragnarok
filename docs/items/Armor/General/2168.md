# Immune Shield (ID: 2168)

## Description
> Very unique square-shaped shield with a special jewel that protects against physical power.  
> For each Refine Level equal to or greater than +5:  
> Decreases damage received from Neutral element attacks by 1%.  
> (The increase is limited to Refine Level 12)  
> Refine Level +7:  
> Decreases damage received from attacks of the Neutral element by 5%.  
> Refine Level +9:  
> Decreases damage taken from attacks of Fire, Earth, Water, and Wind element by 5%.  
> Type: Shield  
> DEF: 55  
> Weight: 70  
> Armor Level: 3  
> Requirement: None
