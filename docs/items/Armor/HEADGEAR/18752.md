# Cursed Book (ID: 18752)

### Description
    A cursed book that can absorb life.
    When attacking, 4% of the damage is absorbed as HP with 4% chance.
    HP absorption increases every 2 Refine Levels
    When attacking, 1% of the damage is absorbed as SP with 1% chance.
    SP absorption increases every 3 Refine Levels
    HP is lost every 5 seconds depending on the Refine level.
    Type: Headgear
    DEF: 3
    Position: Upper
    Weight: 30
    Armor Level: 1
    Requirement:
    Base Level 99
    All Transcendent Classes, except Novice
